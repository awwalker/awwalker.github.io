Bike the City with Citi

How do New Yorkers use Citi Bikes?

~Hover over a bike station to see its common routes or a strand to see the amount of trips between two stations~

Citi bike provides New Yorkers and visitors with a convenient and efficient alternative transportation option. There are hundreds of stations and thousands of bikes available 24 hours a day 7 days a week.


Obviously not all the stations are represented in this diagram. In order to present a clean diagram I had to reduce the number of stations. You can check out the associated blog post here to see the code / reasoning behind this simplification if it interests you, or just take my word that these 17 stations were the most widely used during the month of July, 2013.
